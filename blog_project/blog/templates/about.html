Who Am I?

My name is Chase Weyer, I'm 31 years old, and I enjoy watching anime, playing video games, and visiting new breweries or coffee shops whenever a new one opens up.

I also enjoy travelling to the point that I recently completed an overseas trip to Ireland and the United Kingdom and I can't wait to go back.

I'm originally from the city of Pittsburgh, PA and I moved to Charleston, SC about 2 and half years ago so that I could be closer to my family while I completed my Bachelor's Degree in Information Technology.

I decided to go this route so that I could expand on my previous customer customer support experience with software by learning about different programming languages, frameworks, and software integrations so that I could fully understand software issues and make sure that I could fully resolve software issues and give the best possible user experience as we are all software users at some point and deserve to have the best possible user experience when using a piece of software.
