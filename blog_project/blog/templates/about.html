About

As you might have inferred from my domain name, my name is Chase Weyer. I am a 29 year old video game, anime, funny meme, and baseball enthusiast that just finished working towards my Second Bachelor's Degree in Information Technology at the University of Massachusetts - Lowell as of February 1st, 2017.

I'm sure your thinking "Why a Second Bachelor's Degree?". Well, I enjoyed my original Bachelor's Degree in History from Indiana University of Pennsylvania and all of the work I put towards it, but, over time, it became something that I wanted to pursue as hobby more than a career. As my love of history was becoming more of a hobby, I delved more into my more tech natured hobbies, and I felt it was something I wanted to expand on and possibly turn into a career. Being in cutomer service and holding a previous Tier 1 position only strengthened my resolve. As such, I went back to school to pursue my Second Bachelor's Degree in Information Technology and have not looked back.

Now that I have completed my degree, I hope to expand on my programming skills and eventually become a software engineer/web developer further down the road. Right now, however, I am looking for a full time position that will utilize both the customer service skills developed through my professional career, which can be viewed on my Resume page, and the technical skills that I have developed through college and personal experimentation, which can be viewed on my Portfolio and Skills page.
